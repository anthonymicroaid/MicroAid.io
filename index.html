So what?

Indonesia is at inflexion point

The population is one of the most well connected in the world, with over 80% smartphone penetration and a mobile phone ownership reaching over 110%

Agriculture is at the centre of livelihoods

More than 180 million Indonesians have ties in this sector which makes up 14% of total GDP

Access to finance leads to empowerment

However, there is currently a $73.2bn payment gap of development financing due to unidentifiable farmers and unverifiable collateral

MicroAid App

Our app leverages Blockchain to create identities and verifies collateral for farmers.

It then connects them to finance and a better way of life.
